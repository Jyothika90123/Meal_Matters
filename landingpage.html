About us

"Meal Matters" emerges as a responsive solution to combat these issues, serving as an innovative online platform designed to facilitate food donations and bridge the gap between surplus food providers and individuals in need. The Meal Matters platform is characterized by its user-friendly interface, making it accessible to a wide range of users, from food donors such as restaurants and individuals, to food recipients, including shelters, community centers, and low-income households.

Our Goal

The goal of the food donation platform "Meal Matters" is to build a digital ecosystem that turns surplus food into sustenance for those who need it most, while simultaneously encouraging a sense of social responsibility and group action.
